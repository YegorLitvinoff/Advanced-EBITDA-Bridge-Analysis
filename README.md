# Advanced Financial Risk Assessment & Scenario Modeling (2025-2029)

### 🎯 Project Goal
To build a high-complexity financial model for a 5-year strategic horizon, evaluating the impact of operational risks and macroeconomic factors on the group's EBITDA and profitability margins.

### 🛠️ Tools Used
* **MS Excel** (Scenario Manager, Bridge Analysis / Waterfall charts, advanced sensitivity testing, long-term forecasting).

### 📈 What Was Done (Model Features)
1. **5-Year Base Scenario:** Developed a baseline financial forecast for the 2025-2029 period with a target EBITDA margin of 37%.
2. **Factor-Based Risk Analysis (Bridge Analysis):** Quantified the impact of specific negative drivers: supplier changes, inflation in marketing costs, and bad debt provisions.
3. **Negative Scenario Modeling:** Calculated the "Stress-case" scenario, demonstrating a potential drop in cumulative 5-year EBITDA from 4.6B to 4.1B rubles.
4. **Data Visualization:** Built interactive Waterfall charts to visualize how individual risk factors "erode" the base profit margin.

### 🏆 Key Insights from the Project
* **Risk Sensitivity:** Identified that "Bad Debt Provisions" (accounting for a 4% drop in margin) is the most critical risk factor requiring immediate mitigation.
* **Resilience Testing:** Proved that even under a negative scenario, the business maintains a healthy EBITDA margin of 33%, showcasing strong operational stability.
